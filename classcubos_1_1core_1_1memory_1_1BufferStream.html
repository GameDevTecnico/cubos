◆ BufferStream() [3/3]

BufferStream ( std::size_t  size = 16)

Initializes a buffer stream with a new buffer, initially of the given size. When initialized this way, the buffer stream will own the buffer and will delete it when it is destroyed. It will expand the buffer when needed.
